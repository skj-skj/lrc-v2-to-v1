About LRC Format Conversion

This free online tool converts LRC v2 (Enhanced LRC) files to LRC v1 (Standard LRC) format. Perfect for ensuring compatibility with older media players, karaoke applications, and music streaming services.

What's the difference?

  • LRC v1: Standard format with line-level timestamps only - [00:12.00] Hello world
  • LRC v2: Enhanced format with word-level timestamps - [00:12.00] <00:12.30> Hello <00:12.60> world

This converter removes word-level timing data while preserving line-level synchronization, making your lyrics compatible with virtually all music players and karaoke software.
